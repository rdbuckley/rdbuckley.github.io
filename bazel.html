Bazel Readiness Assessment

Ready for Bazel?

Answering that simple question early is the most important, most practical guidance you can get on the path to your successful Bazel migration. It'll make your life simpler.

How do you know if you're Ready for Bazel?  Easy, get a Bazel Readiness Assessment. Make a small investment in an expert, objective answer that returns a big head start for your migration project. It will save you plenty later.

Newcomers to Bazel may get the impression that a Bazel migration is a daunting task. No value if you start too early; overwhelming and unpleasant if you start too late.

Neither is true.

What is true, and almost always heard about Bazel migration projects, is having readiness knowledge earliest in the process makes a significant difference. How? It improves resource planning, helps communicate expectations, and minimizes surprises.

It's the smart way to start.

You'll get a report at the conclusion of a month's work together. It details how you'll benefit from migrating to the Bazel build system. We'll describe what improvements you can expect, where you might expect regressions, and what resources your migration requires.

Nix + Bazel

Ready for reproducible builds?

Together with Nix, Bazel is integral to delivering byte-for-byte reproducible, composable software systems integrated from hundreds of sources. Tweag's recent Bazel projects include in silico simulations of human physiology, novel static analysis tools for the autopilot software that powers flying autonomous taxis, designing smart contract languages, and improving the performance of third generation blockchains.

Want to know our secret sauce to achieve predictable and reliable success on those high risk projects? Nix + Bazel.

We are co-creators of Nix and care deeply about software correctness and quality. Builds can't be correct unless they are reproducible. They can't be reproducible unless they're hermetic plus deterministic.

Nix + Bazel can do this. And more.

Nix + Bazel = true hermeticity, minimal rebuilds.

Bazel Professional Services

Ready to tune Bazel?

Much of Bazel's power comes from what you can do with it after your initial migration is complete. Bazel is extensible, but not always easily. That's where Tweag comes in.

We make your Bazel better.

Bazel-savvy Tweagers are behind today's boldest innovations in machine learning, distributed computing, and biotech. They add Bazel to these diverse environments too. Then, they tune it for best performance.

We can optimize every phase of your Bazel implementation—but tuning your Bazel is where our combination of experience and expertise may bring you the highest returns.

Why? We know Bazel and its best practices. Let's look at optimizing performance and deploying rules. Those two are significant—and our specialties.

Bazel is fast, fastest with cached builds. We can improve your cache hits by making your builds hermetic—your developers will get work done faster. That may include Nix + Bazel. Blah, blah.

Much of Bazel's strength comes from its ability to define rules. We can write custom Bazel rules to adapt Bazel to your needs. Rules are powerful yet, as Google warns, The cost of writing an inefficient rule may not be evident until it is in widespread use. Our Bazel rules work is commercially-hardened; our open source rules work is here.

Finally, we've been a successful advocate for addressing use-cases at our clients that culminated in core contributions to Bazel. Clients benefit, so does the Bazel community as a whole. Work that supports the use-case is best-positioned to continue to flourish. Everyone wins.
